Helpful answers

Questions brands often ask

What services does Lyons Growth Studio offer?

Lyons Growth Studio offers performance marketing, paid campaign support, creative testing, Shopify support, Klaviyo email marketing, automations, campaign management, and eCommerce growth systems.

What is the difference between performance marketing and eCommerce growth?

Performance marketing focuses on acquisition, paid campaigns, creative testing, and optimisation. eCommerce growth focuses on the Shopify and Klaviyo systems that help turn visitors into customers and customers into repeat buyers.

Can Lyons Growth Studio help if I am not sure what service I need?

Yes. A discovery conversation can be used to understand the current setup, identify the biggest gaps, and recommend the most useful next step.

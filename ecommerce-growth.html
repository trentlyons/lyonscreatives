Helpful answers

Questions brands often ask

What does eCommerce growth support include?

eCommerce growth support can include Shopify improvements, Klaviyo setup, email marketing, flows, automations, campaigns, signup forms, segmentation, and retention-focused customer journeys.

Can Lyons Growth Studio help with Shopify and Klaviyo together?

Yes. The studio is built around connecting Shopify and Klaviyo so the store, email flows, customer data, and marketing campaigns work together more clearly.

What Klaviyo flows can be supported?

Common Klaviyo flows include welcome series, abandoned checkout, abandoned browse, post-purchase, winback, customer thank-you, review request, and nurture flows.

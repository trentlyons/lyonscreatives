About Lyons Growth Studio

A creative and growth-focused studio for brands that want stronger visuals, sharper marketing, and a more joined-up digital presence.

Overview

Lyons Growth Studio is currently a one-man band working somewhere between a focused agency and a hands-on freelancer. It was built by Trent Lyons from a passion for helping creative brands blossom online, with practical support across websites, eCommerce, email marketing, and digital growth.

Based in Suffolk, the studio is rooted locally but built with wider ambitions: to support Shopify and Klaviyo users wherever they are, from independent UK brands to global eCommerce businesses that need clearer systems, sharper campaigns, and a more joined-up customer journey.

How projects are approached

Projects are handled in a flexible, efficient, and personable way. The goal is not to force every brand through the same cookie-cutter process, but to understand what is actually needed, build a clear plan, and keep momentum without making the project feel heavier than it needs to be.

For example, a simple Shopify website build with Klaviyo integration could include:

  • Discovery call to understand the brand, products, goals, and current setup
  • Project plan with agreed priorities, timelines, responsibilities, and next steps
  • Shopify store build or refinement, including structure, pages, styling, and key customer journeys
  • Revision rounds to fine-tune copy, layout, imagery, products, and overall experience
  • Klaviyo integration, signup forms, core flows, email templates, and basic segmentation
  • Progress review calls to keep decisions moving and avoid surprises
  • Final checks, launch preparation, handover training, and project close-off

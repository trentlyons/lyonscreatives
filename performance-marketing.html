Why it matters

Most ad accounts do not fail because of one bad setting.

They usually fail because the offer, creative, campaign structure, landing experience, and follow-up systems are not working together. Performance marketing should connect those pieces, not treat ads as a standalone task.

  • Clearer campaign structure and testing priorities
  • Creative angles built around customer buying triggers
  • Reporting that leads to actual next steps

Helpful answers

Questions brands often ask

What does performance marketing include?

Performance marketing can include paid ads management, campaign planning, creative testing, offer testing, reporting, optimisation, and improving the path from attention to revenue.

Does Lyons Growth Studio only run ads?

No. The work looks at the wider customer journey too, including campaign messaging, creative, landing pages, eCommerce touchpoints, and retention opportunities.

Is performance marketing suitable for early-stage brands?

It can be, but the best route depends on the store, offer, budget, tracking, and current customer journey. The first step is usually to review what is already in place.

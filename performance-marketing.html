Why it matters

Most ad accounts do not fail because of one bad setting.

They usually fail because the offer, creative, campaign structure, landing experience, and follow-up systems are not working together. Performance marketing should connect those pieces, not treat ads as a standalone task.

  • Clearer campaign structure and testing priorities
  • Creative angles built around customer buying triggers
  • Reporting that leads to actual next steps
